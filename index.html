A minimalist pastebin service. Paste your code, or write some!
We also support & render Markdown!

You may also:
  • Hold Ctrl+V to submit clipboard contents immediately
  • Drop text or a file here to insert it
  • Hold Ctrl to see all available keyboard shortcuts
  • Submit command output from any shell with  | nc qbin.io 90
    (pro-tip: click that text, then click the scroll wheel in your terminal to paste)

By submitting any content to qbin.io, you accept our guidelines and privacy policy and confirm that your document complies with them.
